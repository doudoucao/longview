# Research Guidelines

> Project-specific guidance for Longview research tasks.

Longview is a deep value investing community and structured investment research platform. Research work must be evidence-first, current, auditable, and careful about the boundary between investment research process design and investment advice.

## Pre-Research Checklist

- Read the active task's `prd.md`, `design.md`, and `implement.md`.
- Read all files in this `research` spec directory.
- Use current web or source verification before making claims about "latest", "leading", "state-of-the-art", product capabilities, pricing, regulations, or market structure.
- Use Firecrawl as the default tooling for web search, page scraping, local document parsing, crawling, GitHub/paper research, and browser interaction when research needs external content.
- Prefer primary sources and clearly label when a claim comes from marketing, media coverage, benchmarks, or inference.
- Preserve intermediate raw materials or a reproducible source index before synthesizing. Do not rely only on the final answer.
- Do not write personalized investment advice or unsupported buy/sell/hold conclusions.

## Language

Research artifacts may be written in Chinese or English based on the user's request. Source notes and citations should preserve original source names and dates.
